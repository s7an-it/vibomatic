# Product Vision (Canonical)

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Last Updated:** YYYY-MM-DD  
**Status:** Canonical vision document

---

## 1) North Star

One-sentence core outcome. Prioritize product outcome quality over volume metrics.

## 2) Problem Statement

Define the specific collaboration/product problems the system solves and why they persist.

## 3) Who We Serve

Define target users and execution traits. Avoid broad "everyone" language.

## 4) Value Proposition

List concrete differentiators, not implementation details.

## 5) In-Scope Product Reality (Current)

List shipped capabilities and current operating constraints.

## 6) Boundaries and Non-Goals

List what the product is not, and scope boundaries that prevent drift.

## 7) Product Principles

Define operating principles used to evaluate roadmap and tradeoffs.

## 8) Success Definition

Define one north-star metric plus supporting metrics and anti-metrics.

## 9) Evidence Anchors

List primary source files and source-precedence order for future grounding.

## 10) Vision Editing Protocol

Define proposal-first workflow, evidence requirements, and conflict handling.

## 11) Decision Log

Track dated, major vision decisions and migrations.

## 12) Forward-Looking Areas (Exploration, Not Commitment)

Capture future exploration themes without turning them into implied commitments.
